2. **Title Overlay Image**: `Firefly__3d%20and%20clarity%20175685%20jKf%20(5).png`
   - Source: `C:\Users\adary\Downloads\`
   - Destination: `public\Firefly__3d%20and%20clarity%20175685%20jKf%20(5).png`

3. **Background Video**: `firefly_gears.mp4`
   - Source: `C:\Users\adary\Downloads\`
   - Destination: `public\firefly_gears.mp4`

## Quick Copy Commands:

```bash

cp "/c/Users/adary/Downloads/Firefly__3d and clarity 175685 jKf (3).png" "public/"
```

After copying these files, the website will have:

- The video background playing automatically
- The title overlay image displayed on top of the video
